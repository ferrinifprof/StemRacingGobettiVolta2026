THE PROJECT

Il Progetto

STEM Racing Italy è una competizione multidisciplinare che coinvolge team di studenti nella progettazione, costruzione e gara di mini vetture di Formula 1 ad aria compressa.

Ogni squadra, composta da 3 a 6 membri, lavora come una vera scuderia professionale, occupandosi di tutte le fasi: dal design alla stampa 3D, fino al business plan e alla ricerca sponsor.

Il percorso culmina con competizioni nazionali, dove i team si sfidano per dimostrare velocità e qualità del progetto.

SPONSORS

Gli sponsor

PRORASO

Proraso is an Italian company founded in Florence, well known in the personal care and shaving industry. It produces grooming cosmetics with carefully developed formulas and selected ingredients. It provided financial support for our project. We chose it for its strong reputation and product quality. Its product durability and short supply chain help reduce waste and environmental impact.

CAPP PLAST

Capp Plast is a company focused on plastic processing and industrial component production. It provided the boxes used to transport our machines. It supported the logistical part of our project. We chose it for its expertise and production efficiency. It helps reduce waste and optimize material use in industrial processes.
