OUR GOALS

Our Mission

Our goal is to excel in the national STEM competition by combining expertise in design, aerodynamics, and marketing. We aim to develop a winning F1 model while fostering teamwork and technical skills that bridge the gap between school and the professional racing world.

OUR INSTITUTE

The School

ISIS Gobetti-Volta is a leading technical and scientific high school in Florence. The school actively supports the STEM Racing project, providing the facilities and advanced technologies required to transform our designs into real-world prototypes.

Where We Are
