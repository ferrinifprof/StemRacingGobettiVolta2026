THE PROJECT

The Project

STEM Racing Italy is a multidisciplinary competition that involves student teams in the design, construction, and racing of compressed-air powered miniature Formula 1 cars.

Each team, consisting of 3 to 6 members, functions like a professional racing stable, managing every phase of the project: design and model development, aerodynamic analysis, 3D printing and prototype manufacturing, marketing and communication strategies, business plan development, and sponsorship research.

The project combines Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) with design, team management, and entrepreneurship skills. The journey culminates in national competitions, where teams challenge each other with their cars on official tracks to demonstrate speed, innovation, and project quality.
